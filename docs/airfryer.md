## Air Fryer

### Air Fryer specific configuration fields
- none

Available optional Air Fryer controls:

- `airFryerExtraControls`: Enables all extra air fryer controls. Default: `true`.
- `airFryerActionControls`: Adds Pause Cooking, Resume Cooking and Cancel Cooking buttons when supported. Default: `true`.
- `airFryerCustomPropertyControls`: Adds preheat, appointment time, appointment time left, food quantity and turn-pot controls when supported. Default: `true`.
